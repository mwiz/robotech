Corg mini wizard mode
----------------------

Corg mode is qualified after all 4 main modes have been played.

When qualified mystery shot blinks blue and Corg insert is lit solid blue.

Corg mode starts when mystery shot is hit.

RULES
-----
Timed mode - 60 seconds with ball save.  

Time is added at spinner determined by spinner level.

Complete Invid bank of targets 3 times, ramp comes down.

Shoot ramp and ball enters Reflex Point to defeat Corg

Shoot either Rand or Rook shot to add a play into play.

Defeat Corg Value = (200000 * current_player.ramp_shot_multiplier) * current_player.pf_multiplier
