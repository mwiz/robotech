This is a timed mode (60 seconds) and starts off as a 2 ball multiball with ball saver.

Complete top lanes (R-O-B-O) 4 times to complete mode.

After initial ball save is over the Rook shoot will be flashing to add-a-ball with ball saver.

Completing top lanes adds 10 seconds to timer.

Spinner hits adds seconds to timer (determined by spinner level)

SCORING

1st Invid destroyed - 20,000 * playfield_multiplier
2nd Invid destroyed - 30,000 * playfield_multiplier
3rd Invid destroyed - 40,000 * playfield_multiplier
4th Invid destroyed - 50,000 * playfield_multiplier

Total = 140,000 points

Bonus = ((time left * 500) * balls_in_play) * playfield_multiplier
